My Concert Experiences

I love attending concerts and experiencing live music! Here are my last three concert experiences that were truly unforgettable.

Concert 1: Prateek Kuhad at Thalia Hall

I've been following Prateek Kuhad since 2018, and this summer, I finally had the chance to meet him at his concert at Thalia Hall. The music was soothing and created an incredibly memorable experience.

Concert 2: Diljit Dosanjh at Allstate Arena

Diljit Dosanjh made history at Coachella. His performance at the Allstate Arena was electrifying, blending traditional Punjabi music with modern beats. It was an amazing show full of energy!

Concert 3: Post Malone in United Center

Post Malone's concert at the United Center was my first live concert after moving to Chicago, and it was a fantastic introduction to the city's music scene. His performance left a lasting impression.
